About SnapVault



SnapVault is a lightweight project management system designed to help you organize, track, and store your projects with ease.

What is SnapVault?

SnapVault allows users to create projects, upload images, track progress, and manage everything in one place using localStorage (no backend required).

Features

  • Project creation and editing
  • Image upload and gallery support
  • Status tracking (active, urgent, completed)
  • LocalStorage-based persistence
  • Search across all projects
