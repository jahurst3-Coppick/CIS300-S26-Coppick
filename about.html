About SnapVault



SnapVault is a streamlined project management system built to help you organize, track, and store your projects with ease.

What is SnapVault?

SnapVault lets you create projects, upload images, track progress, and keep everything organized in one place. It's designed to be simple and intuitive, making it easy to add new projects, update details, and its collaborative tools ensure that teams can work together smoothly and efficiently.

Features

  • Project creation and editing
  • Image upload and gallery support
  • Status tracking (active, urgent, completed)
  • LocalStorage-based persistence
  • Search across all projects
  • Built for easy collaboration and team alignment
